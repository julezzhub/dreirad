about fundraising support

Three People, three Wheels, three thousand kilometers, and all that in - you guessed it - two weeks.
That's the concept of the Rickshaw run. What was originally designed as an urban taxi will be raced all the way across the Indian subcontinent. We'll spend a day learning how to drive and repair engineering's highest life form, and then take this glorified lawnmower from A (Kochi) to B (Jaisalmer) in order to raise money for charity. Given the inadequacy of our vehicle, there's not much sense in planning ahead. So: no fixed route, no support - apart from your charitable help and loving thoughts that is. If you want to become a part of our trip, be it through your name, face or flag on our beautiful three-wheeler, check how to support us and our selected charities below.
