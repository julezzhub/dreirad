DREIRAD
about fundraising support

Three People, three Wheels, three thousand kilometers, and all that in - you guessed it - two weeks.
That's the concept of the Rickshaw run. What was originally designed as an urban taxi will be raced all the way across the Indian subcontinent. We'll spend a day learning how to drive and repair engineering's highest life form, and then take this glorified lawnmower from A (Kochi) to B (Jaisalmer) in order to raise money for charity. Given the inadequacy of our vehicle, there's not much sense in planning ahead. So: no fixed route, no support - apart from your charitable help and loving thoughts that is. If you want to become a part of our trip, be it through your name, face or flag on our beautiful three-wheeler, check how to support us and our selected charities below. We will visit our local charity Make a Difference in Bangalore just before we kick off our charity race in Kochi.

FUNDRAISING

Cool Earth works alongside indigenous villages to halt rainforest destruction. Local people stand to lose the most from deforestation but the most to gain from its protection, which is why they are the forest’s best possible custodians. All Cool Earth partnerships are community-owned and led. By developing local livelihoods, their mission is to end the cycle of deforestation entrenching villages into further poverty. They create strong, self-determining communities instead of dependency.

Make a Difference India mobilises young leaders towards ensuring equitable outcomes for children in need of care and protection. Their long term goal is to change how both system and families care for and invest in children, targeting stable middle-class adult outcomes for all the children they work with.

HOW YOU CAN SUPPORT US

There's tons of ways you can support us - through kind words, thoughts and prayers, cheering us on, sharing our campaign with your friends, and of course through your personal donations. After all, this is a fundraiser, and 100% of your contributions will go towards our two wonderful charities.
To sweeten the deal for you, we thought of some ways we could make all of you become a part of our journey. So, beyond our eternal gratitude, every donor will have their name on our Rickshaw and our team outfits.
If you want to go above and beyond, everyone donating 20€ or more will get a postcard and some team stickers delivered straight to their door, and every donation over 100€ will receive a photo album and an authentic Indian t-shirt straight from a local market on top of that - and we'll call you up to congratulate you on your fantastic fashion sense.
If somehow, be it with a group or by yourself, you find yourself commemorating our little three-wheeler with a donation of 300€ or more, we would probably be lost for words at first, and then go on to commemorate *you* by letting you pimp our rickshaw. Add a flag (preferrably HSVs) or a face (preferrably yours) to our vehicle, which will accompany us for the entirety of the trip.
So please, be the Ted to our Marshall and Lilly, the Harry to our Ron and Hermoine, the third wheel for us and our Rickshaw. Join the Dreirad Fund and support Cool Earth and MAD India.
